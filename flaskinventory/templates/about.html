Inventory Management

The feature provides businesses with accurate and real-time information about the quantity of products they have in stock, making it easier to make informed decisions about product reorders and restocking.
This helps to avoid stockouts, overstocking, and other inventory-related issues that can negatively impact a business's profitability.

Demand Forecasting

The ML-based demand forecasting model feature works by analyzing historical sales data and identifying patterns and trends that can help predict future demand.
The model uses advanced machine learning algorithms to learn from this historical data, which enables it to make accurate predictions about future demand, even for products that have limited sales data.

Waste Management

Connecting with nearby NGOs is easy with our website, which facilitates the connection between restaurants and NGOs. In addition to helping the community, reducing food waste can also save restaurants money.
By reducing overproduction, using portion control, and implementing efficient storage techniques, restaurants can significantly reduce the amount of food waste they generate, which translates to cost savings.
